# WeeLMat accepted redesign

Reference: `weelmat-accepted-redesign.png`

## Locked direction

- Editorial learning-studio composition with tactile paper and document depth.
- Forest `#173F2A`, brand green `#236130`, warm gold `#D6A73D`, paper `#F6F0E7`, white, and ink `#142019`.
- Lora display typography with Raleway UI/body typography.
- Compact dark-forest header, restrained architectural photography without a color wash, and asymmetric layouts.
- Homepage focal point: oversized promise plus a layered Weekly Learning Matrix document.
- Role dashboard focal point: guided role rail plus a changing dashboard preview; Teacher is active initially.
- Motion metaphor: source paper becomes an organized week. Use transforms, opacity, and route/progress drawing only.
- No generic card wall, glassmorphism, decorative blobs, infinite animation, long scroll-jacking, or oversized empty sections.

## Motion tokens

- Content entrance: `0.42–0.52s`, ease `[0.22, 1, 0.36, 1]`.
- Tactile interaction: spring stiffness `360–400`, damping `28–32`.
- Repeated-item stagger: `0.04–0.065s`.
- Reduced motion: render all content in final state and disable scroll-linked translation/rotation.
